## Module Structure (10 Modules)

### Module 1: General Organic Chemistry (GOC)
**Duration:** ~8 hours | **Priority:** CRITICAL

**Lessons (4 lessons):**
1. **Bonding in Organic Compounds** (50 min)
   - Hybridization (sp, sp¬≤, sp¬≥)
   - Sigma and pi bonds
   - Bond lengths, bond angles, bond energy
   - Covalent character and polarity

2. **Electronic Effects - Part 1** (45 min)
   - Inductive effect (+I, -I)
   - Electromeric effect (E+, E-)
   - Hyperconjugation
   - Applications to acidity/basicity

3. **Electronic Effects - Part 2: Resonance** (50 min)
   - Resonance structures
   - Resonance energy and stability
   - Mesomeric effect (+M, -M)
   - Aromatic resonance

4. **Reactive Intermediates and Mechanisms** (55 min)
   - Carbocations, carbanions, free radicals
   - Carbenes and nitrenes
   - Reaction mechanisms (SN1, SN2, E1, E2, addition, elimination)
   - Arrow pushing conventions

**Quizzes:** 4 quizzes, 30-35 questions total
**Key IIT JEE Topics:** Hyperconjugation, resonance comparison, stability order, mechanism identification

---

### Module 2: IUPAC Nomenclature
**Duration:** ~6 hours | **Priority:** HIGH

**Lessons (3 lessons):**
1. **IUPAC Basics - Alkanes, Alkenes, Alkynes** (45 min)
   - Parent chain selection
   - Numbering rules
   - Substituent naming and prefixes
   - Multiple bonds (ene, yne)
   - Cyclic hydrocarbons

2. **Functional Group Nomenclature** (50 min)
   - Priority order of functional groups
   - Alcohols, aldehydes, ketones
   - Carboxylic acids and derivatives
   - Amines, nitro compounds
   - Multi-functional compounds

3. **Complex Nomenclature - Aromatic & Substituted** (40 min)
   - Benzene derivatives (ortho, meta, para)
   - Common names vs IUPAC names
   - Polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons
   - Heterocyclic compounds

**Quizzes:** 3 quizzes, 25-30 questions total
**Key IIT JEE Topics:** Multi-functional molecules, priority rules, common name conversions

---

### Module 3: Isomerism
**Duration:** ~7 hours | **Priority:** CRITICAL

**Lessons (3 lessons):**
1. **Structural Isomerism** (45 min)
   - Chain isomerism
   - Position isomerism
   - Functional group isomerism
   - Metamerism, tautomerism
   - Counting isomers

2. **Stereoisomerism - Part 1: Geometrical** (50 min)
   - E-Z nomenclature
   - Cis-trans isomerism in alkenes
   - Cyclic compounds
   - Oximes and imines

3. **Stereoisomerism - Part 2: Optical** (55 min)
   - Chirality and asymmetric carbon
   - R-S nomenclature (CIP rules)
   - Enantiomers and diastereomers
   - Optical activity, specific rotation
   - Meso compounds, racemic mixtures
   - Fischer projections

**Quizzes:** 3 quizzes, 30-35 questions total
**Key IIT JEE Topics:** Chiral center identification, R/S assignment, meso compounds, optical activity calculations

**Lessons (3 lessons):**

   - Physical properties, boiling points
   - Free radical halogenation (mechanism)
   - Conformational analysis (Newman projections)
   - Ring strain in cycloalkanes

2. **Alkenes and Alkynes** (55 min)
   - Preparation methods (dehydrohalogenation, dehalogenation)
   - Electrophilic addition reactions (HX, X‚ÇÇ, H‚ÇÇO, H‚ÇÇSO‚ÇÑ)
   - Markovnikov's rule and anti-Markovnikov (peroxide effect)
   - Ozonolysis, hydroxylation
   - Polymerization

3. **Aromatic Hydrocarbons - Benzene** (50 min)
   - Aromaticity (H√ºckel's rule)
   - Electrophilic aromatic substitution (EAS)
   - Nitration, sulfonation, halogenation, Friedel-Crafts
   - Directive influence (o/p directors vs m-directors)

**Quizzes:** 3 quizzes, 25-30 questions total
**Key IIT JEE Topics:** Markovnikov additions, ozonolysis products, EAS mechanisms, directive effects
